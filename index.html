I like coding

I made this site with the help of this online class. I love creating moving parts, writing copy, solving problems, and plotting how a user experiences a website. I'm just starting out. So many possibilities!

I write

I have a B.A. in English. I'm a writer. I also write music. I toured extensively in a pretty successful satire band called Birdcloud. I have three solo records and a synthwave project called You Drive.

I run

I run 100 miles a month. I love running related tech and apps. I organized a run club with my friends. Prioritizing my health and reconnecting with my body has changed my life.

I crush memes

I ran a record-smashing social media campaign over the years with my band. Now I make memes. It's the most captivating and efficent way to communicate an idea. Life shouldn't be too serious.
